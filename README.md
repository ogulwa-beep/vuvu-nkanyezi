# VUVU ✦ NKANYEZI — The Starborn Resonance

> *Zibekezelele — Inspired, patient art that endures like stars*

A mobile-first African-inspired first-person game of music, balance, and ancestral resonance. Collect star-seeds, compose celestial music, and balance the energies of Umsindo and Isithunzi beneath a Zulu night sky.

## 🌐 Play Live

[Play on GitHub Pages](https://YOUR_USERNAME.github.io/vuvu-nkanyezi/)

## 🎮 Controls

### Mobile (Landscape)
- Left thumb drag — move
- Right thumb drag — look
- ✦ button — collect star-seed
- 🎵 button — open Star Map
- ✏️ button — sketch resonance
- ⚡ THUNDER / 🌊 RIVER — shift balance

### Desktop
- Click — lock cursor
- WASD — move
- Mouse — look
- E — collect
- G — Star Map
- Q / R — balance
- Space — sketch

## 🌟 Objective

Collect all 5 star-seeds, arrange them in the Star Map, and balance Umsindo and Isithunzi until the centre star glows gold.
